Register Your CARES Species

CARES registrations are associated with Species Profiles declared in the CARES Priority List.

To register your fish, you must select your CARES Species from the Priority List.

 

1. Navigate to CARES Species (top menu) and use filter and search options to find your species.

2. Click on the Species Name to load the species page.

3. Scroll to the bottom of the page and click the Register This Species button.
