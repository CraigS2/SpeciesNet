How to Register your CARES Species

To register your fish, select your CARES Species from the Priority List and click the 'Register Species' button.

1. Navigate to CARES Species (top menu) and use search options to find your species.

2. Click on the Species Name to load the species page.

3. Scroll to the bottom of the page and click the Register This Species button.
