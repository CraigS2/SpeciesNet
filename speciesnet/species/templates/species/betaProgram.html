Beta Program

AquaristSpecies.net is a new website focused on one key benefit: connecting Aquarists according to the Species they keep.

Try it it's easy as 1, 2, 3!

  1. Create an account with a username, email, and password
  2. Select the Species you keep. Click on the 'I keep this fish!' option
  3. Fill in a few fields describing your fish. Provide as little or as much detail as you like.
