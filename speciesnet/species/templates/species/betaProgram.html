AquaristSpecies.net is a new website focused on one key benefit: connecting Aquarists according to the Species they keep.

Try it it's easy!

Simply create an account with a username, email, and password and select the species you keep. Click on the 'I keep this fish!' option and fill in a few fields describing your fish. It's meant to be quick and easy. Provide as little or as much detail as you like.
