About Us

Welcome to a new site connecting Aquarists according to the Species they keep.

  • Find aquarists keeping the fish you want.
  • Easily contact them!
  • Share the fish you keep.
  • Let others know you have fry available.
  • Make it easy for aquarists to find you!
  • Share your contact info or keep it private - you choose.

For an overview of species features please visit the How It Works page.

For club members interested in starting a new BAP Program the new ** beta ** BAP feature allows configuring species points by Genus and optionally species levels, with an emphasis on CARES species through use of a CARES multiplier. See the BAP Overview.

Once you have an account and are ready to add your species you can use the Add My Species Guided Tutorial

Please join our community.

  • Share the species you keep.
  • Share your whole fish room or just your favorites.
  • Add photos and log notes for the species you keep.
  • Add an optional Species Log for more detailed notes and additional photos.
  • Maintaining a CARES Species? Collaborate with others using a Species Maintenance Group.
  • AquaristSpecies.net is a free open-source solution by Aquarists - for Aquarists.
  • Hobbyists only please! Not for business use.

For those keeping rare, hard-to-find, and endangered (CARES) species please note that you can create a Species Maintenance page and collaborate with fellow hobbyists. Add notes and photos to document your Species Maintenance journey and share husbandry techniques and best practices. For more information see the CARES Overview

AquaristSpecies.net is a croud-sourced community. To succeed we need your help growing the community.
