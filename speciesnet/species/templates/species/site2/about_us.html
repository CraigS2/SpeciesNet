About Us

Welcome to the new CaresSpecies.org site!

CaresSpecies.org is a beta site supporting the CARES Preservation Program. This program encourages aquarium hobbyists to help preserve at-risk species by dedicating tank space for CARES species in their home aquariums. If you are a CARES fishkeeper or a CARES liaison for a local club: Please consider joining our beta program!

This site provides CARES Fish Species details and registration support for species declared on the CARES Priority List.

CaresSpecies.org partners with AquaristSpecies.net to link aquarists keeping CARES species to the CARES Preservation Program. Individuals and CARES Liaisons can use their AquaristSpecies.net accounts to manage their species including CARES Registration and quarterly reporting. To participate in the CaresSpecies.org beta program create an account on AquaristSpecies.net and add your CARES species. If you are a CARES liaison please contact the site admin at aquaristspecies@gmail.com to set up your club and participate in our beta program.
